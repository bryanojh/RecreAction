Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I book the event space?

A: Use the Bookings page to select an available slot and complete your booking.

Q: Can I cancel my booking?

A: Yes, please contact us at least 48 hours in advance for cancellations.

Q: How far in advance do I need to book the space?

A: We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance to ensure availability.

Q: What amenities are included with the event space?

A: The event space comes with chairs, tables, and basic AV equipment. Additional equipment can be rented.
